Capy Orange Macro Recorder

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

Overview

Capy Orange Macro Recorder ("the App") is a desktop productivity tool that allows users to record, manage, and replay keyboard and mouse macros. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

✅ We do not sell your personal data. We do not track your behavior. We collect only what is strictly necessary for authentication.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following information from your Google account:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture
  • A unique Google account identifier

No other personal data is collected from your Google account. We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, contacts, or any other Google services.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected via Google Sign-In is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing your account in the App
  • Authenticating your identity when you log in
  • Displaying your name and profile picture within the App

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is stored in the us-east-1 region and protected using industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

Macro recordings and workflows you create are stored locally on your device and/or in your Supabase account. We do not analyze or process the content of your macros.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth – for user authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase – for secure data storage (Supabase Privacy Policy)

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Capy Orange Macro Recorder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
